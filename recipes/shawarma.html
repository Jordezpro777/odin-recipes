Shawarma

Description

An exotic traditional dish from the middle east culture, a blend of meats, spices and seasons rolled in dough and baked to perfection. A must try for all food lovers.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Beef
  • Spices
  • Seasons
  • Dough

Steps

  1. Prepare the dough
  2. Season the meat
  3. Roll the meat in the dough
  4. Bake the dough
  5. Enjoy your meal
