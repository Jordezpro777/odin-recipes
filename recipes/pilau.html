Pilau

Description

A traditional dish from the Indian culture, a blend of rice, meats, spices and seasons cooked to perfection. A must try for all food lovers.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Beef
  • Spices
  • Seasons
  • Vegetables

Steps

  1. Prepare the rice
  2. Season the meat
  3. Cook the meat
  4. Mix the rice and meat
  5. Enjoy your meal
